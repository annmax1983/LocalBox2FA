Privacy Policy

LocalBox2FA · Last updated: July 2026

TL;DR: LocalBox2FA does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All processing happens fully locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Ever.

1. Data Collection

LocalBox2FA does not collect any personal information, browsing data, or usage analytics. We do not use cookies, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

2. How It Works

  • Input — You enter a TOTP secret key directly in the extension popup
  • Processing — TOTP codes are generated using the Web Crypto API (HMAC-SHA1) entirely in your browser
  • Storage — Saved secrets are stored in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device
  • Export — Exported files are saved directly to your local downloads folder

3. Permissions

LocalBox2FA requests no special permissions. It does not access your browsing data, tabs, network, or any other browser API beyond the basic popup functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

LocalBox2FA does not connect to any third-party services, APIs, or external servers. The extension operates entirely offline.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

6. Changes

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on our repository.
